Recipe #1

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken breast 500g
Olive oil 2 tbsp
Garlic minced 3 cloves
Paprika 1 tsp
Salt To taste
Black pepper ½ tsp
Lemon juice 1 tbsp
Fresh parsley chopped 2 tbsp

Step 1: Preheat your oven to 200°C (390°F). Line a baking tray with parchment paper.

Step 2: In a bowl, mix olive oil, minced garlic, paprika, salt, black pepper, and lemon juice to create the marinade.

Step 3: Coat the chicken breasts evenly with the marinade and place them on the prepared baking tray.

Step 4: Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is thoroughly cooked and has a golden exterior.

Step 5: Garnish the baked chicken with fresh parsley before serving.

Recipe #2

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 200g
Olive oil 3 tbsp
Garlic sliced 2 cloves
Red chili flakes 1 tsp
Cherry tomatoes halved 1 cup
Basil leaves fresh ¼ cup
Parmesan cheese grated ½ cup
Salt To taste
Ground black pepper ¼ tsp

Step 1: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add spaghetti and cook according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside, reserving 1 cup of pasta water.

Step 2: While the pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add sliced garlic and red chili flakes. Sauté for 1-2 minutes until the garlic is golden but not burnt.

Step 3: Add the halved cherry tomatoes to the pan and cook for 5-7 minutes, until they start to soften and release their juices.

Step 4: Toss in the cooked spaghetti, adding a bit of reserved pasta water if needed to help the sauce coat the pasta evenly. Cook together for 2-3 minutes.

Step 5: Remove from heat, and stir in fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.

Step 6: Serve hot, garnished with more basil and Parmesan if desired.

Recipe #3

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Large eggs 4
Avocado 1
Salt To taste
Black pepper ¼ tsp
Chives finely chopped 1 tbsp

Step 1: Fill a saucepan with water and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to a low simmer and gently lower the eggs into the water using a spoon. Cook for 6-7 minutes for soft-boiled eggs or 9-10 minutes for firmer yolks.

Step 2: While the eggs are cooking, cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl. Mash the avocado with a fork and season with salt and black pepper.

Step 3: Once the eggs are cooked to your liking, use a slotted spoon to remove them from the water and place them in a bowl of ice water to stop the cooking process. After a minute, peel the eggs carefully.

Step 4: Spread the mashed avocado onto plates or toast if desired. Cut the soft-boiled eggs in half and place on top of the mashed avocado. Season with a little more salt and pepper.

Step 5: Garnish with chopped chives and serve immediately for a nutritious and quick meal.
